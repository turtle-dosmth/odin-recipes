Sweet Restaurant Slaw

1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix 2 tablespoons diced onion ⅔ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip) ½ cup white sugar 3 tablespoons vegetable oil 1 tablespoon white vinegar ½ teaspoon poppy seeds ¼ teaspoon salt Gather the ingredients. Combine coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl. Whisk salad dressing, sugar, vegetable oil, vinegar, poppy seeds, and salt together in a medium bowl until blended. Pour dressing over coleslaw mixture and toss to coat. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
